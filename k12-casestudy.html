Case Study

Elevate K-12 is passionate about creating high-quality teaching and learning opportunities for teachers and students. Their mission is to make high-quality instruction available to all students, irrespective of where they live, bringing equality to education. They take pride in creating value and opportunities for children and women. Get ready for the new classroom!

elevatek12.com

The Challenge

I was approached by the company to redesign and give a fresh take to the current website that serves as a purely informational resource to current and prospective clients for the company.



The overall challenge was to present the company's services as an "experience". The current website was overloaded with information on landing pages, and for new visitors, it was hard to gauge what the company was offering in a nutshell.

The company wanted to meet a tight deadline of three weeks to complete the project and go-live. I had my work cutout.

elevatek12.com

Users & Audience

The target users of the site are teachers and education administrators seeking remote certified teachers, standardized curriculum, and paraprofessionals to meet long-term shortages in the classroom.

elevatek12.com

Team & Role

This effort was purely a UI design effort on my part. I collaborated with the company's Brand Manager to prioritze content to meet the tight deadline.

elevatek12.com

Wireframing & Planning

The structure would follow a similar hierarchy to the current site.

The objective of the new site was focused on creating a seamless path and have users focus on one concept at a time, therefore allowing ideas to be consumed at a reasonable pace.

elevatek12.com

User Interface Design

We wanted to keep the site familiar, yet focus on the new approach. I decided to keep the primary green and secondary purple colors, but used as accent features rather large splashes of color across the page.

elevatek12.com

Outcome

The site is currently under developement and is pending a new launch date. I was able to meet the company's deadline to provide the new design. It was well received, and the company hopes this will give them the leading push in the industry.
